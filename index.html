ABOUT BK

Welcome to Burger king – a family owned and operated business.Having grown up in NYC and Philadelphia, we fell in love with the hearty sandwiches,made fresh on the spot and the bagels that are uniquely New York.My family and I decided to call Medina, Ohio home several years ago and we craved the flavors we grew up with back east.Those cravings inspired us to open the New York Bagel Deli. We’re bringing the best of the east coast home to Medina.

So prepare your taste buds for some east coast goodness and get ready to gorge yourself with giant portions of the finest and freshest ingredients.You’ll soon be saying Fuhgeddaboutit to the other sandwich shops. Give us a try today!
